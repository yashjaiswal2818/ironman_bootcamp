Stark Defense Decision System

This stage evaluates participants' analytical reasoning and decision-making abilities by simulating how Stark Industries' automated defense system determines operational responses during combat situations. Participants must analyze battlefield conditions and determine the most appropriate Iron Man suit deployment and tactical action based on multiple inputs. The goal is to demonstrate clear logical thinking and situational awareness, similar to how an intelligent system like J.A.R.V.I.S. would operate.

Scenario

Stark Industries operates a defense monitoring system that constantly scans for threats. When a threat is detected, the system must automatically decide which Iron Man suit should be deployed and what action should be taken by analyzing battlefield parameters.
