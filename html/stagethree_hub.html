Design Task

Create a single screen combat Heads-Up Display (HUD) that helps Iron Man monitor and track enemy targets during a mission. Your design should focus on clarity, situational awareness, and fast decision-making, ensuring that the most critical information is easy to identify during combat. The design should reflect the advanced technological style of Stark Industries.

Required UI Components

  1. 1.
    Target Tracking Reticle — A visual targeting system used to lock onto enemies. Must indicate: current target, target lock status, target position relative to Iron Man.
  2. 2.
    Threat Level Indicator — Visual system showing severity of nearby threats (Low / Medium / High). Use colors, icons, or warning signals.
  3. 3.
    Distance / Range Display — Distance between Iron Man and detected target (meters or logical format). Helps determine engagement strategy.
  4. 4.
    Weapon Readiness Status — Status of combat systems (Ready / Charging / Cooling down / Disabled). Use progress bars, indicators, or system panels.

Design Guidelines

  • Resemble a futuristic combat HUD similar to advanced armor systems
  • Maintain clear visual hierarchy for critical information
  • Use clean layouts and avoid unnecessary clutter
  • Use colors and typography strategically for warnings and combat data
  • Interface should feel advanced, minimal, and technology-driven (Stark Industries style)

Deliverables

  • • Screenshot of the design (optional but recommended)
  • • Optional: Short explanation (max 100 words) describing design approach
