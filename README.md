## The Arc Reactor Pitch (Stage 5)

Imagine full access to Stark Industries labs and tech. Pick a real-world problem. Propose a solution. Present clearly.

**5-Slide Format:**
1. **Problem Definition** — What, who, why, data
2. **Problem Impact** — Economic, environmental, social
3. **Technology Solution** — Your idea, what makes it unique
4. **How It Works** — Step-by-step, key tech, features
5. **Expected Outcome** — Efficiency, savings, impact

*Example topics: AI disaster response • Clean arc-reactor energy • Medical nanotechnology*
